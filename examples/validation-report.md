### TAR PIT WARNING

This idea triggers tar pit detection. AI meeting notes have been attempted by 20+ well-funded companies. Otter.ai raised $63M, Fireflies raised $21M, Grain raised $12M. None has achieved dominant market position, yet new entrants keep arriving.

**Critical question:** What is fundamentally different about your approach? "Better AI" is not sufficient - every competitor claims this. A valid answer would be: proprietary data from a specific industry vertical, unique integration that creates switching costs, or a fundamentally different business model (e.g., outcome-based pricing tied to meeting productivity metrics).
