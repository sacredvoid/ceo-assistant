# CEO Operating System

Cadences, templates, and rituals for running a startup. Combines EOS, OKRs, and modern operational frameworks.

---

## Weekly Cadence

| Day | Ritual | Duration | Purpose |
|-----|--------|----------|---------|
| Monday | Metrics review | 60 min | Flight-check: numbers, thresholds, decisions |
| Monday | L10 meeting (EOS) | 90 min | Issue identification, discussion, solution |
| Tue-Thu | 1-on-1s with directs | 30 min each | Coaching, unblocking, relationship |
| Tue/Thu | Deep work blocks | 3-4 hrs | Strategic thinking, no meetings |
| Friday | CEO update email | 30 min write | Team + investor alignment |

## Monthly Cadence

| Ritual | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Roadmap review | Are we building the right things? |
| Budget/forecast variance | Are we spending as planned? |
| Investor update email | Formal metrics + asks (/ceo-board-update) |
| Skip-level meetings | Bypass hierarchy, get ground truth |

## Quarterly Cadence

| Ritual | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| OKR setting + Rock reset | Recalibrate the operating plan (/ceo-okrs) |
| All-hands meeting | State of the union, team alignment |
| QBR | Deep dive on each function |
| Board meeting | Governance, strategic guidance |
| Pre-mortem | Stress-test next quarter's plan |

## Annual Cadence

| Ritual | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Annual planning offsite | Strategy, budget, org design |
| Lock board meeting dates | Set all 4 quarterly meetings |

## David Sacks' "The Cadence"

Complete startup OS from PayPal/Yammer ($1.2B acquisition).

- **CEO Dashboard:** Max 30 rows company-wide. 5-7 metrics per leader.
- **Weekly:** Review metrics, unblock, decide. Leaders come prepared.
- **Monthly:** Roadmap, forecast, budget variances.
- **Quarterly:** OKRs, priorities, recalibrate.

---

## Weekly CEO Update Email

Write Friday, send Monday 9-10 AM. 5 sections:

1. **Top wins** (3-5 bullets with metrics)
2. **Key metrics** with trends (same metrics every week)
3. **What I'm thinking about** (business impact)
4. **People spotlights**
5. **Challenges / asks** (specific, actionable)

---

## 1-on-1 Meeting Framework

Weekly, 30 min. Employee owns 80% of agenda.

**Core Questions:**
1. "How's life outside work?"
2. "Top priorities this week?"
3. "Recent win and recent wish-you-handled-differently?"
4. "Want more or less direction from me?"
5. "What skills do you want to build?"
6. "How's the team dynamic?"
7. "Am I giving enough feedback?"
8. "What should I change?"

**CAMPS Assessment (1-10):** Certainty, Autonomy, Meaning, Progress, Social Inclusion.

This is coaching, not a status update.

## All-Hands Structure

Quarterly, 60-90 min.

1. State of the Union (CEO overview)
2. Customer stories
3. KPI overview + dept updates (3-4 min each)
4. Shoutouts
5. Open Q&A

---

## Board Meeting Prep

- Schedule all 4 a year in advance
- Materials 5-7 days before
- Prep: 8-12 hours (3:1 ratio)
- Structure: 1-page TL;DR, 1-page deltas, supporting evidence
- Time: 60% forward strategy, 40% retrospective

## Founder Mode (Chesky / Paul Graham)

1. Functional org (all functions to CEO)
2. CEO reviews every important piece of work
3. Presence, not absence
4. Skip-level engagement across org
5. Requires MORE systems to stay in details without bottlenecking
