Capabilities

Everything a startup CEO needs, encoded into Claude Code workflows with live research and real data.

V

Idea Validation

15-criteria scoring rubric with parallel research agents. Tar pit detection. Industry-specific benchmarks. GO/NO-GO recommendation.

$

Financial Modeling

14 calculators: ROI, CAC, LTV, runway, valuation, burn multiple, Rule of X. Generates .xlsx spreadsheets.

P

Pitch Deck Generation

Sequoia 12-slide format with live competitor research. Quality-checked against 20 common pitch mistakes.

C

Competitive Intelligence

Live web scraping of competitor websites, pricing, funding, reviews. Feature matrix and positioning map.

CEO Operating System

EOS L10, OKRs, Rocks, weekly cadences, 1-on-1 templates, board prep, decision logs. Sacks' Cadence.

M

Metrics Tracking

Persistent JSON store. Monthly snapshots with trend analysis. Alerts for runway, churn, and unit economics.
