# Customer Persona Generator

Create data-informed customer personas using research.

## Input
Ask: "Who is your target customer? Share any customer data, interview notes, or assumptions."

## Step 1: Research
If user lacks customer data, use WebSearch:
- "[industry] buyer persona demographics"
- "[product category] user research"
- Check Reddit, forums for real discussions about the problem space

## Step 2: Build Persona (1-3 personas)
For each:

### [Persona Name] - [Archetype]
**Demographics:** Age, role, company type/size, income, location
**Bio:** 2-3 sentence day-in-the-life
**Quote:** "Something they'd actually say about the problem"

**Goals:** What "winning" looks like
1. [Goal]
2. [Goal]

**Pain Points:** Top frustrations
1. [Pain with severity: High/Medium/Low]
2. [Pain]

**Current Solutions:** What they use today and why it's inadequate

**Buying Behavior:**
- How they discover: [channels]
- Decision process: [solo/team/committee]
- Budget authority: [yes/no, approval needed]
- Objections: top 3 reasons they'd say no

**Channels:** Where they spend time (social, communities, events)

## Step 3: Validate
- Is this specific enough to guide product and marketing decisions?
- Are pain points evidence-based or assumed?
- Does this match who's actually buying (or would buy)?

## Follow-up
Offer: "Want me to (a) create an interview script to validate this persona (/ceo-interview), (b) build a GTM targeting this persona (/ceo-gtm), or (c) generate ad copy for this audience?"
