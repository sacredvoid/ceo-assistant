# Hiring Plan & Job Description Generator

Create hiring plans, job descriptions, interview scorecards, and compensation models.

## Input
Ask: "What do you need? (a) Full hiring plan, (b) Job description for a specific role, (c) Interview scorecard, (d) Compensation benchmarking"

## Option A: Hiring Plan
1. Ask for: company stage, current team size, next 12-month goals, budget
2. Generate prioritized hiring roadmap:

| Priority | Role | Timeline | Salary Range | Equity Range | Why Now |
|----------|------|----------|-------------|-------------|---------|

Equity benchmarks:
- First 5 hires: 0.5-2% each
- Hires #6-20: 0.1-0.5%
- Post-Series A: 0.01-0.1%
- Standard: 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff

## Option B: Job Description
Generate:
- Title and level
- Company description (3 sentences)
- Role summary (what you'll own)
- Responsibilities (5-8 bullets, outcome-focused)
- Requirements: Must-have vs Nice-to-have
- Compensation range + equity
- Why join now (what makes this moment exciting)

## Option C: Interview Scorecard
- 5 screening questions
- Technical assessment criteria (role-specific)
- Culture/values assessment (3-5 questions)
- Scoring: 1-5 per criterion with rubric
- Red flags checklist
- Hire/No-hire decision framework

## Option D: Compensation
WebSearch for current market rates:
- "[role] salary [city/remote] 2026"
- Check levels.fyi, Glassdoor, Pave data
- Factor in stage discount (10-40%) + equity premium

## Follow-up
Offer: "Want me to (a) draft an org chart, (b) create onboarding checklists, or (c) build interview question banks for more roles?"
