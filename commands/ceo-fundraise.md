# Fundraising Strategy

Plan your fundraising round - how much to raise, from whom, with what materials.

## Input
Ask: "What stage are you at? Current metrics (MRR, users, growth rate)? How much runway do you have?"

## Step 1: Round Sizing
Using formulas from @ceo-assistant/docs/financial-calculators.md:
- Calculate current burn rate and runway
- Funding needed = (Monthly Burn x Target Runway) x 1.25 buffer
- Map to round type:
  - Pre-seed: $50-500K (10-15% dilution)
  - Seed: $500K-3M (15-25% dilution)
  - Series A: $5-20M (20-30% dilution)

## Step 2: Valuation Estimate
- Pre-revenue: Berkus or Scorecard method
- With revenue: Revenue multiples from @ceo-assistant/docs/financial-calculators.md
- Show pre-money, post-money, founder ownership after round

## Step 3: Investor Research
WebSearch for relevant investors:
- "[industry] [stage] investors 2026"
- "[industry] seed fund portfolio"
- Recommend: angels vs VCs vs accelerators based on stage

## Step 4: Materials Checklist
- [ ] Pitch deck (/ceo-pitch-deck)
- [ ] Financial model (/ceo-spreadsheet)
- [ ] One-pager / executive summary
- [ ] Data room contents (cap table, financials, legal docs, metrics)
- [ ] SAFE/note terms decided

## Step 5: Timeline
- Month 1: Materials prep, warm intro collection
- Month 2-3: First meetings, partner meetings
- Month 3-4: Term sheets, due diligence
- Month 4-5: Legal closing

## Output
Complete fundraising plan with round sizing, valuation range, dilution model, investor targeting, materials checklist, and timeline.
