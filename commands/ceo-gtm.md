# Go-to-Market Strategy

Build a complete GTM plan with channels, pricing, and launch timeline.

## Input
Ask: "Describe your product, target customer, pricing (if decided), and launch timeline."

## Step 1: ICP Definition
Define Ideal Customer Profile:
- Demographics/firmographics (company size, industry, role)
- Pain points and current workarounds
- Buying behavior and decision process
- Where they discover new tools

## Step 2: Channel Research
WebSearch for channels in this market:
- "[industry] customer acquisition channels"
- "[competitor] marketing strategy"
Load CAC benchmarks from @ceo-assistant/docs/industry-benchmarks.md

Recommend top 3 channels:
| Channel | Est. CAC | Timeline | Effort | Best For |
|---------|----------|----------|--------|----------|

Reference: 25 channels with benchmarks in @ceo-assistant/docs/validation-frameworks.md

## Step 3: Pricing Strategy
Based on competitor research and value delivered:
- Recommend model: freemium, free trial, paid-only, usage-based
- Suggest specific price points with rationale
- PLG if <$100/mo self-serve, Sales-Led if >$1K/mo enterprise

## Step 4: 90-Day Launch Plan
**Pre-launch (Weeks 1-4):** Audience building, waitlist, content, partnerships
**Launch week:** Product Hunt, HN, Reddit, X, LinkedIn, email list
**Post-launch (Weeks 6-12):** Channel optimization, retention focus, expansion
